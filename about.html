Our Mission: A Home Away From Home for Veterans

At Veteran-BNB, our mission is to provide a welcoming, peaceful, and affordable retreat for veterans and their families. As a retired U.S. Air Force veteran, I created this space to honor those who have served and offer them a place to recharge while enjoying everything Central Florida has to offer.

Located in a premium resort just minutes from Disney World and Universal Studios, our 4-bedroom, 3.5-bath townhome is designed with comfort, accessibility, and fun in mind. With resort-style amenities like a lazy river, pools, jacuzzi, arcade, gym, and more, you’ll enjoy a vacation that truly serves you.

Why We Exist

After years of active duty, I realized many vacation properties were not tailored for veterans—especially those with disabilities or larger families. We aim to fix that by offering a home where veterans are treated with dignity, and their families can relax without restrictions.

What Makes Us Different

  • Veteran-owned and operated with military values
  • Pricing transparency — no surprise fees
  • Disability-friendly amenities
  • Proximity to top attractions
  • Direct booking — avoid 3rd-party fees

You're Not Just a Guest — You're Family

Whether you’re visiting on leave, enjoying retirement, or treating the family to something special — Veteran-BNB is here for you. Join a growing community of heroes who deserve more than just another hotel room.
